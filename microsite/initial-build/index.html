From Stage to Studio: The Journey of a Performer Who Does It All.

James Corden’s career spans from writing and starring in the BAFTA-winning Gavin & Stacey, to hosting The Late Late Show, where Carpool Karaoke became a global sensation. His path bridges British theatre, Hollywood, and late-night television — always with humor and heart. Learn more by exploring his works below!
