Overview

From 2007 to 2010, Corden co-starred in his own series, the BBC Three sitcom Gavin & Stacey. He co-wrote the series with his Fat Friends co-star Ruth Jones; Corden and Jones played the friends of the title characters, with Corden starring as Smithy. The series proved popular and was well-received critically.

Critical Success

  • 2007 British Comedy Awards: Best Male Comedy Performer (Corden) & Best New British Television Comedy (Gavin & Stacey)
  • 2008 BAFTA Television Awards: Best Male Comedy Performance (Corden) & Audience Award for Programme of the Year
  • 2008 British Comedy Awards: Best TV Comedy
  • 2010 National Television Awards: Most Popular Comedy Programme
